About Us

Get to know more about our team and mission

Our Purpose

The Idoma Youth Development Initiative is a community-driven movement committed to uplifting and empowering young people of the Idoma nation through education, entrepreneurship, leadership training, and community engagement.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create sustainable opportunities for Idoma youths to grow, lead, and contribute meaningfully to society. We believe in harnessing local talent and promoting innovation, mentorship, and personal development.

Our Vision

To build a vibrant and self-reliant generation of Idoma youths who are equipped with the skills, confidence, and support they need to shape a better future for themselves and their communities.
