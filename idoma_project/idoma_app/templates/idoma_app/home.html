IDOMA DEVELOPMENT INITIATIVE

"Reviving Our Land, Empowering Our People"

Welcome Message

We welcome all new members to the Idoma Youth Development Initiative (IYDI). You've joined a community of passionate individuals working to develop and empower Idoma youths.

Despite our rich culture and many educated, talented sons and daughters, Idoma land remains underdeveloped. It's time we change that—together.

Our Mission

To educate, empower, and unite the people of Idoma—at home and in the diaspora—for the sustainable development of our land through leadership advocacy, job creation, and cultural preservation.

Our Vision

A vibrant, self-reliant, and developed Idoma land where youth thrive, culture is preserved, and our people live with dignity and opportunity.

Our Objectives

  • Good Leadership Advocacy: Champion responsible, transparent governance across Idoma land.
  • Youth Employment & Empowerment: Promote industrial farming, entrepreneurship, and skills development.
  • Crime & Cultism Prevention: Redirect youth from crime through positive engagement and support.
  • Infrastructure Development: Push for improved roads, hospitals, power supply, and industries—starting with Otukpo.
  • Education & Training: Organize programs to equip our people with practical skills and knowledge.
  • Diaspora Mobilization: Unite Idomas abroad to bring ideas, investment, and support home.
  • Cultural Preservation: Promote our heritage, traditions, and identity as a source of strength and pride.
  • Correct Past Mistakes: Learn from history to chart a better future for generations to come.

What We're Doing Now

  • Launching community industrial farming
  • Planning youth training programs
  • Building partnerships across sectors
  • Promoting the Idoma identity
